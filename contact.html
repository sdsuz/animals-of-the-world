Koala Warriors of the World and How We Can Help

The famous "Home of the Crocodile Hunter" Australia Zoo, based in Beerwah, Queensland, with its very own animal hospital, which opened in 2004, provides medical assistance to injured wild Koalas as well as its own Koalas. The zoo, established in 1970 by the Urwin family, boasts many species of animal and works tirelessly for conservation.
